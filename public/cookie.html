What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device when you visit them. They help websites remember your preferences, enhance performance, and deliver a personalized experience. Cookies also help us ensure your account security and enable certain core functionalities.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for login, navigation, and system security.
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect usage data to help us improve user experience and optimize site performance.
  • Preference Cookies: Store your settings such as language, theme, and saved details.
  • Advertising & Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver tailored promotions and measure ad performance.
  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until deleted or expired, remembering preferences for future visits.

Managing Cookies
